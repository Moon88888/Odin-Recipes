Hot Nutty Irish Coffee


Fresh and tasty

This is a great after-dinner coffee. I like to add a mixture of cinnamon and sugar to the rim before I make them. We sometimes use green sugar.

Enjoy!

Ingredients & Tools required

  • ½ tablespoon white sugar, or as needed
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or as needed
  • 1 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur (such as Baileys®)
  • 1 fluid ounce hazelnut liqueur (such as Frangelico®)
  • ¾ cup brewed coffee, or more to taste
  • ¾ cup brewed coffee, or more to taste

The process!

  1. Mix sugar and cinnamon together on a flat dish. Wet the rim of a mug and dunk into the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  2. Pour Irish cream and hazelnut liqueur into the mug.
  3. Add coffee.
  4. Top coffee with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar.
