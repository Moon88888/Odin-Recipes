Chocoholic's Nightcap


A rich and sweet drink... Very easy to make!

Enjoy!

Ingredients & Tools required

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 fluid ounce hazelnut liqueur (such as FrangelicoÂ®)
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 tablespoon Irish cream liqueur

The process!

  1. In a small saucepan, heat milk until simmering. While milk is heating, combine cocoa powder and sugar in a mug.
  2. Stir in hot water to form a paste.
  3. Stir in the hot milk
  4. Add the Irish Cream, stir and serve.
